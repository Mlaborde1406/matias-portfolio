Design Philosophy

Legacy constraints breed creative solutions

Working within Stellaris's aging engine meant finding elegant workarounds. Every UI element had to balance visual ambition with technical reality—and still feel premium.

Impact & Reach

Complex systems, massive scale

Stellaris demands interfaces that handle thousands of planets, fleets, and diplomatic relations simultaneously—without overwhelming players or tanking performance.

Process

Iterate within constraints

Every UI feature began with technical feasibility checks. I prototyped in Figma, validated with engineering, then iterated in-engine to ensure performance.

Community feedback loops were essential—Stellaris players are passionate and vocal about UX.
